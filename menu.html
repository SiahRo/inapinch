Keeping Food Warm

Food can be provided hot or frozen.

Place the half or full pans in oven at 300Â° for 30 minutes and your food will be ready to eat!

To keep food warm throughout your event, using chafing dishes. We can provide all of the necessary items (chafing dishes and fuel) for an additional service charge.

Chafer Basics

  1. Place water pan in frame
  2. Add one inch of hot water
  3. Place fuel in fuel holder
  4. Light fuel
  5. Warm for 20 minutes
  6. Add hot food
